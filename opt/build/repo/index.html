Meet Milo, a Soft Skills Chatbot from character.ai

Milo is a chatbot from character.ai made to teach users about soft skills. Soft skills are skills that help you understand people and work with them.

You can learn about soft skills with Milo by:

  • Role-playing.
  • Asking questions and get it to explain to you what to do in a specific scenario.

Core scenarios that Milo teaches are based on the most relevant scenarios in social interactions. Such as:

Polite Refusals
Asking For Help
Giving Compliments
Setting Boundaries
Communication Skills
Emotional Intelligence
Building Rapport
Professional Scenarios
Personal Development

COACHING METHODOLOGY

  • 1. First, Milo will ask you which scenario you want to practice.
  • 2. Then, Milo will establish a specific context for the scenario (It's gonna ask you to specify if it needs more context).
  • 3. Milo will roleplay as the other person in the scenario.
  • 4. After 4-6 exchanges, Milo will provide feedback and guidance.
  • 5. Lastly, Milo will end with encouragement and one specific improvement to try.

Now, you may chat with Milo on character.ai. Enjoy!

P.S: You may need to sign in to character.ai to use the chatbot.
